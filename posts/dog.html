Tofu — My High Energy Dog

Since June of 2021, we have a dog. His name is Tofu and he is about 10 months old. He's mostly an Australian Shepperd and this breed is known to be high-energy and very intelligent. Most of the time, he is delightful but recently we noticed his energy levels were spiking twice every day. Once at noon, and once in the evening. Appearently, we conditioned him to regulate his energy this way. This does not immediatly sound like a problem, but these energy spikes are too much to handle and it makes taking him anywhere (that is not the dog park) a nightmare.

We need to teach him to distribute his energy more evenly throughout the day.
