About me

Well, I think it is clear my name is Maxime. Currently, I am studying for my Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence. Within my field of study I am mainly interested in management of data and the use of logic in that context. That interest is expressed vaguely on purpose, as I am perpetually figuring out exactly what I find interesting.

Next to defining myself as a student, I can also define myself as a specifically curious person. My scope of interest is wide and sparse. At this stage of my life I am still figuring out what unites them — if they unite at all. A list of things I like so spend my time doing follows:

  • music: playing the piano, learning about music theory
  • cooking
  • reading: some classics, some fantasy, some literary fiction, some non-fiction and so on…
  • growing plants

This list is incomplete and in no particular order. I'm also not claiming to be an expert, or even good, in any of these things. My time is spend mainly by persuing my interests slowly. The only reason I do any of this is because I want to.
